The Empire Strikes Back

The second movie in the original Star Wars theory takes the viewer back to the galaxy far far away. Three years after the destruction of the Death Star the Empire probes the galaxy in search for the Rebel Alliance. One probe locates a small rebel base on the hostile ice planet Hoth. Join Luke, Leia and Han in their heroic fight against the evil galactic empire.
